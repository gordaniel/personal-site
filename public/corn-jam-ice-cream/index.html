Corn Jam Ice Cream

This is by far my favorite ice cream I've ever made. When I was invited to a corn themed party (crazy) by my girlfriend, the decision was made for me that I was gonna recreate the sweet corn raspberry jam from this creamery in Cambridge.

The ice cream really surprised me. I cannot write a real recipe to this for obligatory reasons, but you'll figure it out if you make it.

I modified this recipe for Philadelphia style ice cream base, but I had to remove some of the sugar since we were adding brown sugar roasted kernels.

...also maybe some of the salt but I added more salt in during churning so none of this really matters I can't even look at the news anymore without crying.

Since I couldn't find raspberry jam at Stoppy's, I took some strawberry jam and added lemon juice to make it tart. ZOMG this was so delicious. I would recommended making it pretty tart and making sure it's well distributed in the ice cream. Also go pretty liberal with it. Yeah it's that good.

I blended the base to incorporate the corn, and after I just eyeballed the correct amount of liquids to add. While that cooled I played catfishing:

This was a big hit at the corn party and everyone loved me I made so many friends and shared so many laughs.
