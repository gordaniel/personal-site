When I was invited to a corn themed party (crazy) by my girlfriend, the decision was made for me that I was gonna recreate the sweet corn raspberry jam from this creamery in Cambridge.

The ice cream really surprised me. I cannot write a real recipe to this for obligatory reasons, but you'll figure it out if you make it.

I modified this recipe for Philadelphia style ice cream base, but I had to remove some of the sugar since we were adding brown sugar roasted kernels.

...also maybe some of the salt? But I added more salt during churning so none of this really matters I can't even look at the news anymore without crying.

I used some raspberry jam from Stoppy's and added lemon juice to make it tart. ZOMG this was so delicious. I would recommended making it pretty tart and making sure it's well distributed in the ice cream. Also go pretty liberal with it since the ice cream is so rich. Yeah it's that good.
